Project

AI Job Market Analysis

This project examines the geographic distribution of AI-related job postings and compares traditional tech hubs with other metropolitan areas.

The key finding is that AI jobs remain strong in major tech hubs, but a larger share of postings is found outside these hubs when considering the overall distribution.
